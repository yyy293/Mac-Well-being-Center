About Us

The MAC Wellbeing Center supports students through a wide range of extracurricular activities focused on creativity, physical health and emotional wellbeing. We provide spaces, programs and mentors for students to develop skills, make friends and improve mental resilience.

Our Programs

Art & Creativity Club

Team sports, yoga and fitness sessions that build confidence and teamwork.

Mindfulness Workshops

Guided sessions on stress management, meditation and emotional regulation.

Student-led initiatives, volunteering and skills-building activities.
